RubyBlackJack
=============

Brett's Blackjack Casino

House Rules:
* 6 deck shoe
* Dealer hits on soft 17
* Insurance currently not available
* Player cannot hit on 21 (You're welcome)

# Usage

## Starting the game
To launch the game, cd lib/ then ./game.rb

## Controls

* Hit - H or h
* Stand - S or s
* Double Down - D or d
* Split - P or p
